3 Prague, Czech Republic

In Prague, old and new are mixed. The modern highlight of Prague is the Dancing House. And narrow cobblestone streets lead to mysterious Gothic castles and squares. Prague is called the pearl of Europe.

Sights of Prague

  • National Theatre
  • Powder Tower
  • Dancing House

2. Beijing, China
